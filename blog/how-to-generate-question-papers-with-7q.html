How to Generate Question Papers with 7Q (Full Guide)

July 6, 2026 · 4 min read · by Shirandasu Sandeep

From "I have an exam on Chapter 4" to a printable question paper with an answer key — in under a minute.

What 7Q does

7Q generates complete question papers and question banks on any topic — MCQs, fill-in-the-blanks, true/false, short answers and long answers, with a full answer key. It covers Class 1–10, Intermediate (MPC/BiPC/CEC/MEC), every major degree course, CA, CMA, US CMA and competitive exams — in English, हिंदी or తెలుగు.

Making your first paper

  1. Open 7q.7by.in and sign in (Google or Gmail — 30 free credits daily).
  2. Choose your level, course and subject.
  3. Type the topic ("Photosynthesis", "Partnership Accounts — admission of a partner") — or snap photos of your textbook pages and the questions come only from that material.
  4. Pick question types, difficulty and paper length, then tap Generate question bank.

What you get

  • A properly formatted paper with marks per question.
  • A complete answer key — for MCQs, the correct option; for numericals, the worked answer.
  • Export to PDF for printing, and a shelf that remembers your last 20 papers on the device.

Who uses it

Students self-test before exams (the single highest-return study habit there is); teachers and tuition centres produce fresh, non-repeating worksheets; parents make weekly revision tests. Basic papers run on the free daily credits; hard/practical papers and CA·CMA level use the premium AI models with the Pro plan (₹47/month).
