Smart compression, explained

STEP 01

Modern encoding

Your video is re-encoded with H.264 using CRF rate control — bits go where your eyes will notice them.

STEP 02

Resolution scaling

Most videos are watched on screens smaller than they were shot for. Downscaling to 720p is usually invisible on a phone.

STEP 03

Audio slimming

Audio is re-encoded to efficient 96 kbps AAC — transparent for speech and most music.
